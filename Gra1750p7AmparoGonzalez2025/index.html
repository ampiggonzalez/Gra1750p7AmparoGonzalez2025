ABOUT THE MOVIE

Released in 1999, 10 Things I Hate About You is one of the most iconic and beloved teen romantic comedies of all time. The film follows Kat Stratford, a smart, independent high schooler, who couldn't care less about dating, and Patrick Verona, the charming bad boy who's dared to win herheart. What starts as a plan to help Kat's younger sister Bianca date quickly turns into unexpected romance, hilarious high school chaos, and sweet, unforgettable moments that have made audiences laugh, swoon, and fall inlove for decades. With is perfect mix of humor, heart, and the ups and downs of high school life, 10 Things I Hate About You is more than a just a rom-com. It's a timeless, feel-good classic that everyone needs to see at least once.
